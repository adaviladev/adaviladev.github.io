About me

Started my career as a translator, learning languages to speak with people. Now I'm a frontend developer and I learn languages to speak with computers.

Years ago, I embarked on a professional journey to become a translator, immersing myself in the beauty of languages to bridge communication between diverse cultures. Now, I've transitioned into the dynamic world of frontend development, where I harness the power of programming languages to engage in a different form of dialogue – one with machines. Just as I once sought fluency to converse with people, I now master languages to articulate instructions and craft immersive digital experiences. It's a fascinating evolution, where my passion for language continues to guide me, albeit on a new and exciting path.

I love listening to progressive rock/metal and I enjoy playing the guitar.

I have a cat who always reminds me that I should take a break from the screen from time to time.

I speak Spanish, English and understand Portuguese and a little bit of Japanese.
